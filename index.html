Green Tea

The Healthiest Drink on Earth

Is there anything more nutritious than a cup of green tea? While green tea has been enjoyed in China for around 5000 years, it has been slowly introduced into the western world since the 16th century. Over the past 50 years, green tea has become an extremely popular beverage choice for tea drinkers worldwide. The health benefits of green tea are countless.

Did you know?

  • It is loaded with antioxidants and nutrients that improve health.
  • It can improve brain function and make you smarter.
  • It increases fat burning and helps you lose weight.
  • It lowers your risk of getting:
    • Various types of cancer
    • Alzheimer’s and Parkinson’s disease
    • Infections
    • Type II diabetes
    • Cardiovascular disease
